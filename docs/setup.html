Set a Custom New Tab Page in Smoothwall (Firefox iOS)

  1. Open Smoothwall (Firefox iOS) on your iOS device.
  2. Tap the menu 'â˜°' at the bottom-right.
  3. Select Settings.
  4. Tap New Tab under the General section.
  5. Choose Custom URL.
  6. Paste the URL of your chosen tab from the Tab Index page.
  7. Tap < Back then Done to save.

Your new tab page is now set! Open a new tab to see it in action.
